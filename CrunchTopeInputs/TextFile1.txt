then in main routine that calls the (PETSc) chemistry solvers

 amatpetsc = userC%amatpetsc 
 bvec = userC%bvec 
 xvec = userC%xvec

 ksp = userC%ksp

 or CALL PetscFinalize  (to destroy amatpetsc, bvec, etc...)
